Computer Science Principles - MIT App Inventor Project

Duration: September 2018 - October 2018

Objective: Design and program a creative app on MIT App Inventor for a smartphone or tablet

We developed an app that has the capabilities of sending messages (text, emojis, or emoticons) or media (pictures or videos). Select the type of message or take the picture you want to send, and it automatically redirects you to Gmail, where you can send the message/video to any recipient. Our app also allows the user to take a video, save it, and view it on the app at a later time. Using MIT App Inventor, I created a tool that can be used to conveniently and quickly send media or messages.
